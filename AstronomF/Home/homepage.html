Space Exploration

We have been exploring space for quite some time now. To explore space, we don't necessarily have to go directly to space. For decades we have been exploring space in many ways. From sending man-made objects (such as rovers and satellites), taking detailed pictures of distant space objects, to sending astronauts to space. In ancient times, humans can only see space by looking at the stars from the naked eye or through early telescopes. But time changes, and so does our technology. Now, we are currently in the age of space exploration. The age of space exploration began during the cold war till now.
