Our Website

Astronom is a website that provides readers with information about space. This website only has three pages in total. The homepage, the about us page, and the ask us page. The home page provides readers with information about space such as the definition of space and space exploration. After that, there is the about us page (the page you are currently in). This page provides readers with information about Astronom as a website. Lastly, there is the report page. On the report page, readers can report us something and we will review the reports and try our best to adjust the website according to the reports. By reading our website, we hope that readers can know what great things have we achieved as a species in exploring it.
